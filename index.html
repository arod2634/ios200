Welcome to iOS200

In this course student further their knowledge of how to develop and deploy applications for the Apple iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch using iOS SDK and the Xcode Integrated Development Environment. The course will guide students in developing applications for the iOS platform and further knowledge of the Objective-C language and existing constructs that are used in typical application development. Other topics include Data Management, Table Views, iPhone Navigation Controls, the Camera and Gesture Recognizers.
